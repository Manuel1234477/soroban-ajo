## Getting Started

### What is Ajo?

Ajo (also known as Esusu) is a decentralized savings group platform built on Stellar blockchain. It enables communities to:

- Create transparent savings groups
- Contribute fixed amounts on a schedule
- Receive payouts in rotation
- Build financial trust and credit history
- Access emergency funds when needed

### System Requirements

- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Stellar wallet (Freighter recommended)
- Internet connection
- XLM tokens for transactions

### Creating Your Account

1. Visit the Ajo platform
2. Click "Connect Wallet"
3. Select your Stellar wallet
4. Approve the connection
5. Your account is ready!

## Creating a Savings Group

### Tutorial: Start Your First Group

**Time Required**: 5-10 minutes

#### Step 1: Navigate to Groups

1. Click "Groups" in the main menu
2. Click "Create New Group" button
3. You'll see the group creation form

#### Step 2: Fill in Group Details

| Field | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| **Group Name** | Unique name for your group | "Office Savings Circle" |
| **Description** | What the group is for | "Monthly savings for team bonding" |
| **Contribution Amount** | Fixed amount per cycle | 100 XLM |
| **Cycle Duration** | How often contributions are due | 30 days |
| **Max Members** | Maximum group size | 10 members |
| **Currency** | Token to use | XLM or USDC |

#### Step 3: Configure Advanced Settings (Optional)

**Grace Period**: Extra time to contribute without penalty
- Default: 5 days
- Useful for members with irregular schedules

**Penalty Rate**: Fee for late contributions
- Default: 5%
- Helps incentivize on-time payments

**Payout Strategy**: How to distribute funds
- **Rotation**: Each member gets payout in turn
- **Lottery**: Random selection each cycle
- **Merit-based**: Based on contribution history

#### Step 4: Review and Create

1. Review all settings
2. Click "Create Group"
3. Approve the transaction in your wallet
4. Your group is created!

### Example: Office Savings Group

```
Group Name: Office Savings Circle
Members: 10
Contribution: 100 XLM per month
Total Pool: 1,000 XLM per cycle
Payout: 1,000 XLM to one member each month
```

## Joining a Group

### Tutorial: Join an Existing Group

**Time Required**: 3-5 minutes

#### Step 1: Find a Group

**Option A: Search**
1. Go to "Discover Groups"
2. Use search bar to find groups
3. Filter by contribution amount, duration, etc.

**Option B: Invitation Link**
1. Receive invitation from group creator
2. Click the link
3. Group details appear automatically

#### Step 2: Review Group Details

Before joining, check:
- ✓ Contribution amount fits your budget
- ✓ Cycle duration works for you
- ✓ Group size is appropriate
- ✓ Payout strategy aligns with your needs

#### Step 3: Join the Group

1. Click "Join Group" button
2. Review terms and conditions
3. Approve transaction in wallet
4. You're now a member!

#### Step 4: Set Preferences

1. Go to "My Groups"
2. Select the group
3. Click "Settings"
4. Configure:
   - Notification preferences
   - Payment method
   - Emergency contact

## Making Contributions

### Tutorial: Contribute to Your Group

**Time Required**: 2-3 minutes

#### Step 1: Access Your Group

1. Click "My Groups"
2. Select the group
3. Click "Contribute"

#### Step 2: Verify Contribution Details

- Contribution amount
- Due date
- Current cycle
- Your balance

#### Step 3: Make Payment

1. Click "Pay Now"
2. Confirm amount in wallet
3. Approve transaction
4. Contribution recorded!

### Contribution Timeline

```
Cycle 1: Jan 1 - Jan 30
├─ Contribution Window: Jan 1 - Jan 25
├─ Grace Period: Jan 26 - Jan 30
└─ Payout: Jan 31

Cycle 2: Feb 1 - Feb 28
├─ Contribution Window: Feb 1 - Feb 25
├─ Grace Period: Feb 26 - Feb 28
└─ Payout: Mar 1
```

### Late Contributions

If you miss the contribution window:

1. **Grace Period**: Pay with penalty (5% default)
2. **After Grace Period**: Cannot contribute this cycle
3. **Penalty Calculation**: `Amount × Penalty Rate`

**Example**:
- Contribution: 100 XLM
- Penalty Rate: 5%
- Late Payment: 105 XLM

## Receiving Payouts

### Tutorial: Receive Your Payout

**Time Required**: Automatic

#### How Payouts Work

1. **Cycle Completes**: All contributions collected
2. **Verification**: System verifies all payments
3. **Payout Executed**: Funds sent to recipient
4. **Notification**: You receive confirmation

#### Payout Rotation Example

```
Group: 5 Members (A, B, C, D, E)
Contribution: 100 XLM each
Total Pool: 500 XLM per cycle

Cycle 1: Member A receives 500 XLM
Cycle 2: Member B receives 500 XLM
Cycle 3: Member C receives 500 XLM
Cycle 4: Member D receives 500 XLM
Cycle 5: Member E receives 500 XLM
```

#### Tracking Your Payout

1. Go to "My Groups"
2. Select group
3. View "Payout Schedule"
4. See your payout date and amount

#### What Happens If Someone Doesn't Contribute?

- **Incomplete Cycle**: Payout reduced by missing amount
- **Example**: If 1 of 5 members doesn't pay:
  - Expected: 500 XLM
  - Actual: 400 XLM
  - Shortfall: 100 XLM

## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: What is XLM?**
A: XLM (Stellar Lumens) is the native cryptocurrency of the Stellar network. It's used for transactions and fees.

**Q: Do I need a Stellar wallet?**
A: Yes, you need a Stellar wallet to participate. Freighter is recommended for browser use.

**Q: Is my money safe?**
A: Yes. All transactions are on the blockchain and verified by the network. Smart contracts ensure funds are distributed correctly.

**Q: Can I withdraw my money anytime?**
A: You can withdraw after your payout cycle completes. During active cycles, funds are locked for fairness.

### Group Management

**Q: Can I create multiple groups?**
A: Yes, you can create and join multiple groups simultaneously.

**Q: What if a member doesn't contribute?**
A: The payout is reduced by that member's contribution amount. The group can vote to remove non-contributing members.

**Q: Can I change group settings after creation?**
A: Some settings can be modified by the group admin. Major changes require group consensus.

**Q: What happens if the group is cancelled?**
A: All collected funds are returned to members proportionally.

### Payments & Transactions

**Q: What are transaction fees?**
A: Stellar network fees are minimal (typically < 0.01 XLM). Some groups may charge admin fees.

**Q: How long do transactions take?**
A: Stellar transactions typically complete in 3-5 seconds.

**Q: Can I use different tokens?**
A: Yes, groups can accept multiple tokens (XLM, USDC, etc.) with conversion rates.

**Q: What if a transaction fails?**
A: Failed transactions are automatically retried. Contact support if issues persist.

## Troubleshooting

### Connection Issues

**Problem**: "Wallet not connecting"

**Solution**:
1. Ensure Freighter extension is installed
2. Check wallet is unlocked
3. Refresh the page
4. Try a different browser

### Payment Issues

**Problem**: "Insufficient balance"

**Solution**:
1. Check your wallet balance
2. Account for transaction fees
3. Fund your wallet with more XLM
4. Wait for previous transactions to complete

**Problem**: "Transaction rejected"

**Solution**:
1. Check transaction details
2. Verify amount is correct
3. Ensure wallet has enough balance
4. Try again in a few moments

### Group Issues

**Problem**: "Cannot join group"

**Solution**:
1. Verify group is still accepting members
2. Check you're not already a member
3. Ensure you have sufficient balance
4. Contact group admin

**Problem**: "Payout not received"

**Solution**:
1. Check payout schedule
2. Verify all members contributed
3. Wait for cycle to complete
4. Check transaction history

### Account Issues

**Problem**: "Cannot access account"

**Solution**:
1. Clear browser cache
2. Try incognito/private mode
3. Check internet connection
4. Contact support with wallet address

## Best Practices

### For Group Creators

1. **Set realistic amounts**: Consider members' budgets
2. **Clear communication**: Explain all terms upfront
3. **Regular updates**: Keep members informed
4. **Fair policies**: Establish clear rules for penalties
5. **Backup plan**: Have contingency for missing payments

### For Members

1. **Plan ahead**: Budget for contributions
2. **Set reminders**: Don't miss payment deadlines
3. **Communicate**: Inform group of issues early
4. **Track history**: Monitor your contribution record
5. **Build trust**: Maintain good standing

### Security Tips

1. **Secure wallet**: Use strong passwords
2. **Verify addresses**: Double-check before sending
3. **Enable 2FA**: Add extra security layer
4. **Backup keys**: Store recovery phrases safely
5. **Avoid phishing**: Only use official links
